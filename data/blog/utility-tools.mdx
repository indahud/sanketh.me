[getupilink tools](https://tools.getupilink.com) offers different tools for calculations and productivity.

Tools List:
- [Simple Interest Calculator](https://tools.getupilink.com/tools/simple-interest-calculator)
- [EMI Calculator](https://tools.getupilink.com/tools/emi-calculator)
- [SIP Calculator](https://tools.getupilink.com/tools/sip-calculator)
- [Percentage Calculator](https://tools.getupilink.com/tools/percentage-calculator)
- [Bulk URL opener](https://tools.getupilink.com/tools/percentage-calculator)

Users can calculate Simple Interest, EMI, SIP, Percentage with [getupilink tools](https://tools.getupilink.com)

Multiple URLS can be opened at once with it. No extra extensions are needed for this.
